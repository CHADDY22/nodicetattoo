F.A.Q.
How do I book an appointment?
You can browse our artists’ profiles on our website to choose the artist best fit for your project. Each artist manages their own bookings & their preferred means of contact is listed below their bio in their artist page. If you do not have a preference for a particular artist, you can send an inquiry to nodicetattoo@gmail.com and we will pair you with an artist. All of our artists require a deposit to set an appointment.
Do you accept walk-ins?
We do accept walk-ins when our artists have the availability. Please note- an appointment is the only way to guarantee a tattoo. You can walk in during our open hours, Tuesday - Saturday, 12-7pm, however there is always a chance that all our artists are busy for the rest of the day. If that is the case, we can consult with you and then set an appointment for the next availability with that artist.
What is the age requirement for getting a tattoo?
You must be at least 18 years old with valid photo ID to get tattooed. We do not tattoo minors even with parental consent. Valid ID includes: Drivers license, State ID, Passport, and Tribal ID.
How much do tattoos cost?
Pricing can vary based on size, complexity, placement, and artist. Our shop minimum is $100. We are happy to give you a price estimate on any tattoo before booking, and will do our best to work with your budget.
How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?
We always recommend coming well-rested, hydrated, and having eaten beforehand. If you’re coming in for a large tattoo, we recommend bringing food & snacks for the day. It is best to avoid alcohol or drugs the day before and day of your appointment.

Consider the area you’re getting tattooed and dress appropriately. We recommend loose fitting, comfortable clothing. There is always the chance that ink may get on your clothing near your tattoo, so it is best to wear clothing you don’t mind getting stained. You can bring different clothing to change into at the shop before your tattoo.
Is there parking available?
There is free street parking available in the area around the shop. Generally, the most parking available is on Jackson st, and Snyder Ave. Please note that street parking east of 20th st is 2-hr parking. We recommend looking for parking between 20th and 22nd st and accounting for the time it may take to park and walk over.
What is your aftercare advice?
You will need to wash and care for your tattoo daily for approximately 2 weeks. See below for a PDF with specific aftercare instructions. If you have any questions or concerns about the healing of your tattoo, please reach out directly to your artist or to nodicetattoo@gmail.com
