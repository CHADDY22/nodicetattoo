Owner & Artist
Cece is a self-taught tattoo artist and owner of No Dice Tattoo, with over six years of professional experience specializing in fine line black & gray tattoos. While she primarily uses a machine now, Cece still offers hand-poked tattoos for those who appreciate the technique.

Passionate about her craft and community, Cece is thrilled to bring a tattoo shop to the South Philly neighborhood she has cherished for so long.

To book a tattoo with Cece, visit Cecepokes.com or email cecepokesu@gmail.com
