Tattoo Artist
Marco began working in a tattoo shop as an apprentice in 2012 and has been tattooing full time since 2014. Over the ten+ years of work he has learned from professional tattooers, graffiti writers and art professors alike. He since has focused his work on the main three styles of fine line black and grey, classic American, and Japanese style motifs.

Marco most enjoys tattooing strong, striking images and portraying a wide variety of themes that resonate with each client. He enjoys pairing his knowledge of classic imagery and technique to bring the clients idea into reality, with the perfect tattoo.
