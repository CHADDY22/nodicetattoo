Artist
Sullivan has been tattooing for seven years, and in that time has developed a unique take on typical traditional tattooing.  

Simplicity and legibility are on the forefront of his design style, and he loves to work in full color or just black shading to bring it all together. He is happy to do any of the flash he has available or to bring your custom ideas to life- big or small.

His practice is patient, collaborative and communicative, aiming to provide people with a tattoo experience that feels comfortable and empowering.  Being a part of the queer and trans community, Sullivan finds much joy in being a part of the transformation and personal reclamation of queer bodies through the magic of tattooing.
